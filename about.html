About Dataset

The dataset contains shopping information from 10 different shopping malls in Istanbul between 2021 and 2023. Data was gathered from various age groups and genders to provide a comprehensive view of shopping habits in Istanbul.

Variables:

  • invoice_no: Invoice number (nominal)
  • customer_id: Customer number (nominal)
  • gender: Customer's gender (string)
  • age: Customer's age (integer)
  • city: City where the transaction took place (string)
  • product_category: Product category purchased (string)
  • payment_method: Payment method used (string)
  • invoice_date: Date of the transaction
  • shopping_mall: Name of the shopping mall

For more details, visit the dataset source.
